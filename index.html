Top three things to do in Seattle!

Enjoy nearby hikes and outdoor sports

Seattle is known as an outdoor state! Despite the rain, Seattlelites enjoy hiking, running, fishing and other outdoor activities! Being outdoors will always be the best option as you have Mt. Rainier in the background.

Visit Pike Place Market and support small businesses!

Pike place market is filled with tons of small local businesses. There is so much to explore and the food is amazing! The ferris wheel and Wings over Washington is just a small walk from there as well!

Visit the Space Needle and Museums!

Taking a trip to the Top of Space Needle will provide you with amazing views of the city. Underneathe the Space Needle, you will find the Pacific Science Center where you can enter a butterfly kingdom and bring your Einstein out.
