Top three things to do in Seattle!

Enjoy nearby hikes and outdoor sports!

Seattle is known as an outdoor state! Despite the rain, Seattlelites enjoy hiking, running, fishing and more sports! Being outdoors will always be great as you will always have mount rainier in the background.

Visit Pike Place Market and support small businesses!!

Pike place market is filled with small local businesses. There is so much to explore here and really good food! The ferris wheel and wings over washington is just a small walk from here as well!

Visit the Space Needle and Museums!

You have to visit the Space Needle when you come to the city! Taking a trip to the top of the space needle is worth it as you get to see the whole city and its close to many museums!
